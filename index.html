Hi! I'm Amanda Sakuma. I am an editor and writer based in Brooklyn.

For the past decade, I've worked as a journalist covering politics, immigration, and social justice. Most recently, I edited a special series at The Guardian US focused on fossil fuel industry and its role in driving the climate crisis.

My journalism career offered me many exciting opportunities, but now it's time for a new adventure. I'm looking forward to transitioning into the tech world.

In my spare time, I enjoy sewing, playing Minecraft, and tending to my growing collection of indoor plants.

I grew up on a berry farm in Washington state and now live in Brooklyn, NY.
